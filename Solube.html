LIPIDS

Solubility of Oil

1. Mix 1 teaspoon of baking soda in a little amount of water.

2. In a separate container, pour a little amount of ethyl alcohol.

3. In a separate container, pour a little amount of isopropyl alcohol.

4. In each of the containers, pour 1 tablespoon of oil and mix them.

5. Identify the solubility of the oil with each of the different substances.
