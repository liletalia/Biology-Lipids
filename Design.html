LIPIDS

Experiment Proposal

The proposed experiments will be the “Paper Bag Test” for identifying the existence of Lipids in substances, to be followed by a “Solubility of Oil” experiment which will be used to identify the solubility of Lipids as well as their ability to possibly emulsify when in contact with certain substances.

Materials


Paper Bag Test

The following materials are needed:
Paper bag
Dropper / syringe
Light source
Water
Oil
